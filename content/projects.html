eBay and Local Controller Repair Business

Since September of 2024, I have turned a hobby I picked up into a sole-proprietary business. After repairing several video game controllers for myself and some friends, I realized how much waste is created from consumers throwing away expensive controllers due to common issues that could easily and cheaply be solved. In each controller, I install TMR Hall-Effect joysticks that use magnets and sensors that are physically incapable of developing stick-drift and dramatically increase the lifespan of the controller. I have 100+ successful mail-in repairs from all over the US and Canada through my eBay store page, and around 50+ local repairs from sites like Facebook Marketplace. I am currently partnered and in active communication with one of the lead manufactureres of TMR Hall-Effect joysticks, AKNES Hallpi, as well as very active in a global online community of hobby-ists and repair shops in the field.
